top 5 activities

Living Desert Zoo and Gardens

47900 Portola Ave, Palm Desert, Greater Palm Springs

The Living Desert Zoo is home to several desert animals that have adapted to the desert’s harsh conditions.

Palm Springs Air Museum

745 N Gene Autry Trl, Palm Springs, Greater Palm Springs

Rated by CNN as one of the top 14 Air Museums in the World. This facility Gives visitors an up-close look at the legendary fighters and bombers of WWII, Korea, and Vietnam.

Cabot’s Pueblo Museum

67616 E Desert View Ave, Desert Hot Springs, CA 92240

The four-story Cabot’s Pueblo Museum, which opened in 1949, is full of Native American artifacts and art, works made by Cabot himself, and souvenirs from Cabot’s world travels. A large portion of the Pueblo is made out of sun-dried and adobe-style bricks made by Cabot.

Joshua Tree National Park

74485 National Park Dr, Twentynine Palms, CA 92277

Curves and Zig Zags

Palm Springs, CA 92262

Curves and Zig Zags is located in what seems to be a random spot within the Palm Desert of the Coachella Valley. It is a white wall that boasts a length of almost one hundred feet. When the massive art installation is seen from straight on, it looks as if the black markings on the wall and the wall itself are flat perfectly horizontal. However, just one step to the side reveals that isn’t the case at all.

Workshop

Workshop Kitchen + Bar has retrofitted a 90-year old movie theater into a James Beard Award winning restaurant design. The industrial chic interior gives the Class 1 historic El Paseo building a contemporary edge befitting the locale.

Bustling locale offering updated comfort classics for breakfast & brunch in a simple space.

Bistro
