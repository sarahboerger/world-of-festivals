Top 5 places to eat and drink

4coffee soul food

Hrvojeva 9, Split, Croatia

Coffee is food for the soul, and this little café serves exactly that, so if you fancy some of Split’s best Coffee, visit the cozy café of 4coffee soul food to get your fix.

CHOPS GRILL Steak and Seafood

Tončićeva 6, Split, Croatia

If you’re looking for a good steakhouse and want to treat yourself to some luxury food, CHOPS GRILL steak and seafood is well worth a visit. The lavish and sumptuous food makes for a special evening, well worth paying that little bit extra for, give it a go.

Pikanterija

Ulica kralja Tomislava 3, Split, Croatia

Try some of the local cuisine and visit Pikanterija, serving some of the best Croatian style sandwiches and local beers and brews.

To je tako

Ulica Kraj Svetog Ivana 5, Split, Croatia

This popular Mexican restaurant is a delight and serves incredible dishes, including many vegan friendly and gluten free options.

Sexy cow

Zrinjsko-Frankopanska 6, Split, Croatia

Get some tasty fast food close to the festival grounds by visiting Sexy Cow, it's great value for money. This cow is not just sexy, but vegan friendly too!

Top 5 activities

Hop on, hop off - sightseeing busses

All around the city

If you're running low on time, Split has it's fair share of hop on, hop off sightseeing busses, where you can buy a day card and see the city from the bus.

Game of Thrones museum

Bosanska 9, Split 21000 Kroatien

If you’re a big fan of the HBO series Game of Thrones, this museum is just for you. Walk the path of the Stark family through the world renowned tv-series. Get a first hand look at the props, costumes and the behind the scenes work of Game of Thrones.

Explore the Old Split city

Ulica Knezova Nelipica 1A, Split, Croatia

Take a break from the festival and traverse to the old Split city.

Marjan park

Escape from the noise and crows of the festival by taking a walk and looking out over the beautiful ciry of Split in the Marjan park, near the festival grounds.

Fruits square

Trg Brace Radic, Split, Croatia

If you love the hustle and bustle of the market place, then you will love walking through the Fruits Square market. Browse the many stalls and treat yourself to some delightful fresh produce.
