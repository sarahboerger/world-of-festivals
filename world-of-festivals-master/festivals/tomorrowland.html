Top 5 places to eat and drink

‘t Schoon Verdiep

Tuyaertsstraat 26, Boom, Belgium

Set in the beautiful surrounding of a 19th century mansion, ‘t Schoon Verdiep is a timeless classic, the best in fine Belgian dining.

Den Boom

Antwerpsestraat 35, Boom, Belgium

Den Boom is an Exquisite restaurant that serves high quality, classic Belgian cuisine. If you’re looking for a classy meal during your trip, this is the place to go!

Eetcafé ‘t Steencaycken

Hoek 76, Boom, Belgium

'T Steencaycken is a cozy eatery where you can enjoy nostalgic dishes in an authentic setting whilst offering great value for money.

Bistro Ivan

Kaai 22, Boom, Belgium

Another great place to get value for money, Bistro Ivan serve food for every taste, it’s well worth a visit.

New Canton

Leopoldstraat 14-16, Boom, Belgium

New canton is the best in Boom for Asian cuisine, serving a wide variety of delicious Chinese food.

Top 5 activities

De Schorre

Schommelei 1, Boom, Belgium

A landmark within the festival grounds. Here you can walk on the One World Bridge, which features an Avicii memorial, as well as marvelling at the incredible wooden sculptures around the park.

Brick bicycle tour

All around the city

Rent yourself a bike and cycle around the wonderful city of Boom.

Intense

Schommelei 1, Boom, Belgium

Intense is an indoor climbing and activity centre for children and adults, a truly good work out!

De Velodroom

Hoek 129, Boom, Belgium

If you like bikes, and the biking history. Then this biking museum is just for you. It features cycling items, jerseys, books and more.

Gemeentelijk Park Boom

If you want to escape the noise and intensity of the festival for a few hours, visit the tranquil local park and relax and recharge.
