Top 5 places to eat and drink

4coffee soul food

Hrvojeva 9, Split, Croatia

Despite the name, the little café only serves coffee. So if you fancy some of Split’s best Coffee, visit the cozy café of 4coffee soul food to get your fix.

CHOPS GRILL Steak and Seafood

Tončićeva 6, Split, Croatia

If you’re looking for a good steakhouse and want to treat yourself to some luxury food, CHOPS GRILL steak and seafood is a good choice. It’s in the expensive bit of our top 5 list, but give it a go.

Pikanterija

Ulica kralja Tomislava 3, Split, Croatia

If you’re feeling like getting some good quality croatian sandwiches Pikanterija is a good choice. They also offer croatian beers and brews.

To je tako

Ulica Kraj Svetog Ivana 5, Split, Croatia

If you’re looking for a place to get vegan mexican food, you’ve found the right place. They also have gluten free dishes.

Sexy cow

Zrinjsko-Frankopanska 6, Split, Croatia

If you’re looking for affordable fast food close to the festival grounds take a look at Sexy Cow. The name might fool you, but they also have vegan dishes.

Top 5 activities

Hop on, hop off - sightseeing busses

All around the city

If you don’t have all day, split has their share of hop on, hop off sightseeing busses, where you can buy a day card and watch the city from the bus.

Game of Thrones museum

Bosanska 9, Split 21000 Kroatien

If you’re a big fan of the HBO series Game of Thrones, this museum is just for you. Walk the path of the Stark family through the world renowned tv-series. Get a first hand look at the props, costumes and the behind the scenes work of Game of Thrones.

Explore the Old Split city

Ulica Knezova Nelipica 1A, Split, Croatia

Take a break from the festival and traverse to the old Split city.

Marjan park

Take a break from the noise of festivals and the human crowds, by taking a walk and looking over the beautiful Split city in the Marjan park, near the festival grounds.

Fruits square

Trg Brace Radic, Split, Croatia

If you’re a person who likes markets, this place is for you. Take a walk through the Fruits square market or buy some fresh produce to take with you back.
