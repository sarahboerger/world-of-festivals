Top 5 places to eat and drink

‘t Schoon Verdiep

Tuyaertsstraat 26, Boom, Belgium

If you’re looking for fine dining ‘t Schoon Verdiep, is your choice for a nice atmosphere. They serve fine belgian dining.

Den Boom

Antwerpsestraat 35, Boom, Belgium

If you’re looking for fine dining during your stay in Tomorrowland, Den Boom is your choice. It’s a bit on the expensive side, so only do it if you really fancy some fine dining and still have money left.

Eetcafé ‘t Steencaycken

Hoek 76, Boom, Belgium

If you’re looking for cheap delicious food Eetcafé ‘t Steencaycken is a good choice. They have a wide variety of food for all tastes also for vegans.

Bistro Ivan

7Kaai 22, Boom, Belgium

If you’re looking for cheap food, Bistro Ivan is a choice. They have food for most tastes, but only two vegan dishes.

New Canton

Leopoldstraat 14-16, Boom, Belgium

If you fancy some asian cuisine, New Canton is your choice. They serve all kinds of chinese cuisine.

Top 5 activities

De Schorre

Schommelei 1, Boom, Belgium

A landmark on the location of the festival. Here you can walk on the One World Bridge, which features an Avicii memorial. Or you can walk around and see the different statues made out of wood in the nearby area.

Brick bicycle tour

All around the city

There’s also a possibility to rent a bike and cyckle around the city of Boom.

Intense

Schommelei 1, Boom, Belgium

If you’re looking for a workout, you can take a trip to Intense. It’s an indoor climbing park and adventure course for both adults and children.

De Velodroom

Hoek 129, Boom, Belgium

If you like bikes, and the biking history. Then this biking museum is just for you. It features cycling items, jerseys, books and more.

Gemeentelijk Park Boom

If you're tired of all the festival noise, take a walk in the closeby park and relax.
