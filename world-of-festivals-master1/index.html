FESTIVALS MADE EASY

Our job is to help you get comfortable with your overall festival planning!
Whether you are an experiented festival goer, or a newcomer who is curious about which festival is most suitable for you. It is not unusual for festival guests to arrive unprepared. We want to contribute to solve this problem, by making you as prepared as the camping conditions demands, by providing two different checklists for you to be confident in your packing. As the event is preceding, you might feel exhausted and in a need of relaxation. You will find a list of local activites beneath your chosen festival, the list involves activities such as scuba diving, getting a massage or jetskiing. Maybe you just want a bite of delicious food? therefor we offer you a recommended Café and Restaurants list aswell.

We are sharing important information about the festivals in the world, reaching the vast festival community and help it grow by encouraging first time festival attendies to hop on board. We strive to bring you an in depth guide to each of our festivals, giving you information tips and tricks which can benefit in choosing the right festival for you!
