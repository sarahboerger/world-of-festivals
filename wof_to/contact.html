Frequently asked questions

Your question might already have been answered so we've collected some of our most frequently asked questions below. Please read them through, before submitting a question.

Do you sell tickets?

No, we do not sell tickets. For tickets we'll refer you to the festivals own site on their domain. There's links to the different festivals under their section.

I want my ticket refunded

As we're not a part of any of the festivals listed, we can't refund your ticket. Please contact the costumer support of the festival you've been to for refunds.

Why do you not have X Festival listed?

We do not have some festivals listed as they do not want to be on our list. The festival could also be located further down in our list. If you want a festival to be on our list, you can suggest a festival in the form below.

I know of a good festival not listed/ I represent a festival not listed and wish to have it listed on your site, how would I do that?

If you represent a festival you can contact us on notarealemail@wof.com and we can sort it out there. If you want us to reach out to a festival, you can send your recommendation in the form to the right, with the subject "festival recommendation".

Where can I see the lineup?

Unfortunately we do not show the lineup to the festival. If you're interested in one of the festivals, you can go to the festivals own page here and follow the link to their website.

Need help? Feel free to send us a message below

If you have any questions don't hesitate to ask us, we will get back to you as fast as possible.
