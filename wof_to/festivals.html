Festivals

Tomorrowland in Boom, Belgium

Founded by Manu and Michiel Beers in 2005, Tomorrowland is now one of the biggest festivals in the world. Each year the festival has a theme different from the last years.

learn more

Sydney Festival in Sydney, Australia

Established in 1977, Sydney Festival is a major arts festival in Australia's largest city, Sydney. It runs for three weeks every January. It features local and international events.

learn more

Coachella in Indio, California

Co-founded by Paul Tollett and Rick Van Santen in 1999, the Coachella Valley Music and Arts festival features many genres of music, art installations and sculptures.

learn more

Ultra Music Festival Europe in Split, Croatia

Ultra Europe made it's debut as a two-day festival in 2013. Ultra is a multi-venue outdoor electronic music festival and it is part of the Ultra Music Festival's worldwide expansion.

learn more

Boomtown in Hampshire, United Kingdom

Boomtown (also known as Boomtown Fair) is an independent immersive five-day theatrical music festival. The festival has areas with each of their own unique theming, where hundres of actors playing the role of inhabitants.
